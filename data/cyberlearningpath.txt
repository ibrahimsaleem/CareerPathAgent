**Cybersecurity Learning Path**  

1. **Cyber Security Starts Here…**

2. **Fundamental IT Skills**  
   - Computer Hardware Components  
   - Connection Types & Their Function  
     - NFC  
     - WiFi  
     - Bluetooth  
     - Infrared  
   - OS‑Independent Troubleshooting  
   - Basics of Popular Suites  
     - MS Office Suite  
     - Google Suite  
     - iCloud  
   - Basics of Computer Networking  

3. **Hands‑On Practice (CTFs)**  
   - HackTheBox  
   - TryHackMe  
   - VulnHub  
   - picoCTF  
   - SANS Holiday Hack Challenge  

4. **Certifications**  
   - *Beginner*  
     - CompTIA A+  
     - CompTIA Linux+  
     - CompTIA Network+  
   - *Associate*  
     - CCNA  
   - *Foundations*  
     - CompTIA Security+  
   - *Advanced*  
     - CEH  
     - CISA  
     - CISM  
     - GSEC  
     - GPEN  
     - GWAPT  
   - *Expert*  
     - GIAC (various)  
     - OSCP  
     - CREST  
     - CISSP  

5. **Operating Systems**  
   - Windows, Linux, macOS overview  
   - Installation & Configuration  
   - Versions & Differences  
   - GUI vs. CLI navigation  
   - Permissions model  
   - Installing software  
   - CRUD (file operations)  
   - Troubleshooting & common commands  

6. **Networking Knowledge**  
   - OSI Model  
   - Common Protocols & Ports  
   - SSL/TLS basics  
   - NAS vs. SAN  

7. **IP Terminology**  
   - Subnetting fundamentals  
   - Public vs. Private IPs  
   - localhost, loopback, CIDR, subnet mask, default gateway  

8. **Core Network Concepts**  
   - VLAN, DMZ, ARP  
   - VM, DHCP, DNS, NAT, IP, Router, Switch, VPN  
   - LAN, WAN, MAN, WLAN  

9. **Network Topologies**  
   - Star, Ring, Mesh, Bus  

10. **Network Protocols & Services**  
    - SSH, RDP, FTP, SFTP  
    - HTTP/HTTPS, SSL/TLS  

11. **Virtualization Technologies**  
    - VMware, VirtualBox, ESXi, Proxmox  
    - Hypervisor, HostOS vs. GuestOS vs. VM  

12. **Troubleshooting Tools**  
    - nslookup, ipconfig, netstat, ping, tracert, dig, arp, route  
    - Packet sniffers & protocol analyzers (tcpdump, Wireshark)  
    - Port scanners (nmap)  
    - iptables, curl, hping  
    - Forensics tools (FTK Imager, Autopsy, WinHex, memdump)  

13. **Authentication Methodologies**  
    - Kerberos, LDAP, SSO, RADIUS, Certificates, Local auth  
    - Authentication vs. Authorization  

14. **Security Skills & Knowledge**  
    - Hacking tools & exploit frameworks  
    - Defense‑in‑Depth & runbooks  
    - Forensics & incident response (prep, ID, containment, eradication, recovery, lessons learned)  
    - Threat hunting, vulnerability management, reverse engineering  
    - Pen‑test rules of engagement; perimeter vs. DMZ vs. segmentation  
    - Zero Trust core concepts  
    - Compliance, auditors & risk definition  
    - Backups & resiliency; Cyber Kill Chain  
    - MFA/2FA, OS hardening & isolation  
    - IDS/IPS, honeypots; false positives/negatives  
    - Blue/Red/Purple teams  
    - Threat intel & OSINT; handshakes; CIA triad; privilege escalation  
    - Web attacks & OWASP Top 10; malware types  

15. **Cryptography & Frameworks**  
    - Salting, hashing, key exchange, PKI, public vs. private keys, obfuscation  
    - MITRE ATT&CK, Kill Chain, Diamond Model  

16. **Standards & Platforms**  
    - ISO, NIST, RMF, CIS Controls, CSF  
    - SIEM & SOAR  

17. **Offensive Distros & LOLBAS**  
    - ParrotOS, Kali Linux, LOLBAS  

18. **Log Analysis**  
    - Event logs, syslogs, NetFlow, packet captures, firewall logs  

19. **Hardening Techniques**  
    - MAC‑based, NAC‑based access control; port blocking; sinkholes  
    - Group Policy, ACLs; patch management  
    - Jump server, endpoint security  

20. **Secure vs. Unsecure Protocols**  
    - FTP vs. SFTP; SSL vs. TLS; IPsec; DNSSEC; LDAPS; SRTP; S/MIME  

21. **Security Appliances & Concepts**  
    - Antivirus/antimalware, EDR, DLP, HIPS, NIDS/NIPS, host firewalls, sandboxing  
    - EAP vs. PEAP; WPS; WPA/WPA2/WPA3 vs. WEP  

22. **Threat Classification & Tools**  
    - Zero‑day vs. known; APT  
    - VirusTotal, Joe Sandbox, Any.run, urlvoid, urlscan, WHOIS  

23. **Social Engineering & Attack Vectors**  
    - Phishing, whaling, smishing, spam vs. SPIM  
    - Shoulder surfing, dumpster diving, tailgating  
    - Reconnaissance, impersonation, watering‑hole, drive‑by, typo‑squatting  
    - Brute‑force vs. password spray  

24. **Network & Application Attacks**  
    - DoS vs. DDoS; MITM; spoofing; evil twin; DNS poisoning; deauth; VLAN hopping; rogue AP  
    - Buffer overflow; memory leak; XSS; SQLi; CSRF; replay; pass‑the‑hash; directory traversal  

25. **Stakeholders & Communication**  
    - Audience: IT, HR, legal, compliance, management  

26. **Cloud Security**  
    - Cloud vs. on‑prem differences; IaC; serverless; SaaS/PaaS/IaaS  
    - Models: public, private, hybrid; AWS, GCP, Azure  
    - Cloud storage: S3, Dropbox, Box, OneDrive, Google Drive, iCloud  

27. **Programming & Scripting**  
    - Python, Go, JavaScript, C++, Bash, PowerShell  

28. **Keep Learning**  
    - GTFOBins, WADComs
